Fake friends — a note to self 💙

Life is too short to spend time with people who secretly hope you fail.

They ask about your promotion with fake concern. They go silent when good things happen. They always have a 'but' after congratulations.

Choose people who:
— Celebrate your wins like their own
— Defend you in rooms you're not in
— Actually want you to succeed

Your circle should expand your possibilities, not shrink them.

It's not mean to cut people off. It's mean to yourself not to.
"Choose people who choose you back." 💙
